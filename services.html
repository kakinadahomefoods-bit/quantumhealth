How we sequence care

Diagnostics first. Always.

Plenty of clinics will sell you a detox before anyone has established you have anything to detoxify. We won't.

Testing comes first here, and the results decide the protocol — not the other way round. Sometimes that means a programme shorter than you expected. Occasionally it means telling you that what you're dealing with needs conventional medicine and we're not the right people for it. We'd rather say that at the consultation than six days in.

Not sure which you need?

That's the normal starting position, and it's what the consultation is for. Tell us what's been happening. We'll tell you what we'd test, what we'd likely recommend, and what it would involve — before you commit to anything.
