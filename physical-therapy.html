Physical Therapy & Rehabilitation

Personalised rehabilitation programmes designed to support recovery and improve functional outcomes.

Physical therapy and rehabilitation are integral components of a comprehensive treatment plan. At Swiss Regenerative Klinik, we offer individually tailored PT&R programmes that complement your broader protocol — whether you are recovering from injury, managing a chronic condition, or working to maintain functional capacity.

Our Approach

PT&R sessions are designed around your specific clinical presentation and goals. Each programme may include:

  • Therapeutic exercise — prescribed movement patterns to restore strength, mobility, and endurance
  • Manual therapy — hands-on techniques including joint mobilisation and soft tissue release
  • Gait and balance training — for neurological and musculoskeletal conditions affecting movement
  • Respiratory physiotherapy — breathing exercises and airway clearance techniques
  • Pain management strategies — modalities such as therapeutic ultrasound, TENS, and heat/cold therapy
  • Patient education — guidance on self-management, ergonomics, and long-term activity modification

When PT&R May Be Recommended

Our clinicians may recommend physical therapy alongside your core treatment for:

  • Neurological conditions affecting motor function, coordination, or gait
  • Musculoskeletal injuries, post-surgical recovery, or chronic pain
  • Cardiovascular and pulmonary rehabilitation
  • Age-related decline in strength, balance, or mobility
  • Recovery support following intensive treatment protocols
