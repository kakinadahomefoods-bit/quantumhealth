Alternative & Complementary Medicine

Supportive therapies offered alongside your core treatment protocol.

At Swiss Regenerative Klinik, we recognise that healing is not purely biochemical. Our integrative approach includes selected complementary modalities that may support your primary treatment — reducing stress, improving comfort, and supporting the body's own regulatory systems.

Complementary Modalities We Offer

Fine-needle insertion at specific points on the body, based on traditional Chinese medicine principles. Used adjunctively for pain management, stress reduction, and support during detoxification protocols.

Individualised dietary guidance based on your diagnostic results, metabolic profile, and treatment goals. May include therapeutic diets, meal planning, and supplement protocols where indicated.

Guided meditation, breathwork, and stress-reduction techniques. These practices are offered as supportive tools for patients who wish to incorporate them into their recovery.

Selected hands-on techniques including medical massage and lymphatic drainage, offered to support circulation, reduce muscle tension, and complement physical therapy.

Assessment and recommendations based on naturopathic principles — including hydrotherapy, herbal medicine, and lifestyle counselling — integrated with your medical treatment plan.

How We Integrate Complementary Therapies

Complementary modalities are never offered as standalone treatments or as alternatives to evidence-based medical care. They are selected based on your clinical picture and preferences, and always administered alongside — never in place of — your core treatment protocol. Your primary clinician coordinates all aspects of your care to ensure safety and coherence.
