Medical Check-Ups

Comprehensive diagnostics for preventive health and personalised treatment planning.

Our routine medical check-ups are designed to establish a detailed baseline of your health — covering cellular function, metabolic efficiency, cardiovascular status, hormonal balance, and nutritional status. The results form the foundation of any subsequent treatment protocol, and they give us something to measure against as your programme progresses.

Key Components of Our Medical Check-Ups

A comprehensive review of personal medical history, family history of disease, lifestyle factors (diet, activity, sleep, stress), and current medications and supplements.

Vital signs, body composition analysis, musculoskeletal and neurological assessment, and cardiovascular and pulmonary screening.

Complete blood count, comprehensive metabolic panel, lipid profile, inflammatory markers, nutritional panel (vitamins, minerals, amino acids), hormonal profile (thyroid, adrenal, sex hormones, growth hormone axis), cardiovascular risk markers, and oxidative stress markers.

Urinalysis, stool analysis, ECG and echocardiography, bone density scanning (DEXA), and imaging studies (MRI, CT, ultrasound) as indicated.

Telomere length measurement as a marker of biological age, mitochondrial function analysis, and immune profile assessment. These are offered as optional specialised panels.

Following your assessment, you will receive a detailed report with a recommended action plan covering lifestyle modifications, nutritional supplementation, hormone optimisation if indicated, preventive interventions, and regenerative treatment options.
